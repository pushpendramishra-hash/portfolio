Our Services
Industries we serve

Distribution – Food

We help food distributors manage multi warehouse operations with visibility into stock expiry and picking. Voice guided picking and palletization logic reduce errors and speed fulfilment. Transport bidding and routing are integrated to balance cost and service. Forecasting drives procurement so you do not run empty or overstock.

Distribution – High value merchandise

We design controls for high value goods so inventory and shipments remain secured and auditable. Serial and lot tracking ensures traceability across storage and transit. Integrated order workflows enforce approvals and reduce financial leakage. Multi currency and multi company support keeps accounts accurate.

Distribution – Hazardous materials

We implement inventory controls and document handling to meet safety and compliance needs. Special storage and handling rules are encoded into receiving and picking. Labeling and certificate workflows simplify inspections and audits. Automated alerts help prevent storage or transport breaches.

Manufacturing – Processed food

We deliver process manufacturing with recipe management lot tracking and yield calculation. Material consumption and machine runtime feed accurate costing. Expiry aware picking minimizes waste. Shop floor integration gives real time production visibility to operations and finance.

Manufacturing – Fashion apparel

We support size and color variants seasonal planning and multiple bills of material. Demand planning and allocation help balance online and wholesale channels. Production scheduling and subcontractor workflows keep lead times predictable. Returns and repair flows improve the customer experience.

Manufacturing – Custom merchandise

We automate custom order capture from commerce platforms to production and fulfilment. Design approvals and file storage flow into the shop floor. We support drop ship and consolidated billing for franchise networks. Demand forecasts guide raw material buys and reduce rush costs.

Services – HVAC dispatcher

Field service scheduling with offline mobile capabilities keeps technicians productive in the field. Work orders parts pickup and time capture feed finance in real time. Route optimization reduces travel time and improves response. Service histories and warranties speed diagnostics.

Integrations – EDI and omni channel

We build robust EDI mappings for retail partners and keep commerce platforms synced for stock pricing and orders. Order routing supports multiple warehouses and drop ship. Monitoring and alerts help keep integrations healthy. We design retry and exception flows so operations keep running.
